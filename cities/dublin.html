Dublin

A warm, welcoming and fun city ❤

Dublin,the capital of Ireland, is located on the east coast of the country, at the mouth of the River Liffey, which divides the city into two parts. More peaceful and hospitable than London, its quiet streets and easy walking distance allow you to get to know it walking through buildings of 18th century Georgian architecture. Whether you fancy Michelin-star dining, cosy cafés, tasty Irish classics, the best in world cuisine, bustling food markets, or fresh seafood straight from the coast, Dublin's a place with something to suit all tastes. Also, it is home to google's european headquarters, numerous pubs and the famous guinness beer.
