Aveiro

The portuguese venice ❤

Aveiro is a city on the Portuguese west coast next to a lagoon known as the Ria de Aveiro. It is distinguished by its channels navigated by colorful boats (moliceiros) and for its typical sweet, Ovos Moles. This city has always been linked to the sea trade, fishing and salt production. Near the center is Costa Nova with its colorful houses; Barra Beach, with the biggest lighthouse in Portugal, Ílhavo, the land of cod, among many other places worth visiting.
