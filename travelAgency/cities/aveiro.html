The city of lights and love ❤

The ‘City of Light’ has long been the preferred city break destination for doe-eyed couples who come for the unrivalled architecture, the quality of its restaurants and romantic night cruises down the River Seine. But there is more to Paris than just the language of love. This place is also the capital of fashion, haute cuisine and is one of the most culturally rich cities in the world. Paris is full of galleries dripping with artistic masterpieces and streets filled with some of the most iconic buildings in the world..

Have you ever thought about climbing the most famous tower in the world (Eiffel) after visiting the most visited museum (Louvre) and the most famous avenue (Champs-Elysées)? Well, maybe one day it won't be enough. Come with time and also see Notre-Dame, the Sacré Coeur and the Pompidou Centre.
