About us

Bumblebee, a travel agency out of the box

Welcome to Bumblebee, your favourite travel agency. We're dedicated to giving you the very best travel experiences, tailored to your needs and preferences.
Founded in 2021 by Beatriz, Filipa e Rita, Bumblebee ideia began to come to life in Aveiro, during a coffee conversation.
What united us and made us create this company was our shared passion for traveling and the desire to live the best experiences in each country where we go.
We now serve customers all over the world, giving them the same experiences that we have been through and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own work.
We hope you enjoy the experiences thought out in detail as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
