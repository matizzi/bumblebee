About us

Bumblebee, a Travel Agency focused on Experiences

Welcome to Bumblebee! We're dedicated to giving you the very best travel experiences, tailored to your needs and preferences.
Founded in 2021 by Beatriz, Filipa e Rita, Bumblebee ideia came to life in Aveiro, during a coffee conversation.
What made us create this company was our shared passion for traveling and the desire to live the best experiences in each country.
We now serve customers all over the world, giving them the same experiences that we have been through.
We hope you enjoy the experiences thought out in detail as much as we enjoy offering them to you.
If you have any questions or feedback, please don't hesitate to contact us.
